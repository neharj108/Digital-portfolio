Commitment

FabLab Student Agreement

📜 FabLab, SSGMCE is responsible for:

  • Teaching principles and practices of digital fabrication
  • Arranging lectures, recitations, and events
  • Evaluating and providing feedback on student work
  • Offering clear standards for completing assignments
  • Certifying and archiving student progress
  • Supervising class preparation
  • Reviewing prospective students, instructors, and labs
  • Providing central staff and infrastructure
  • Fund-raising for costs not covered by tuition
  • Managing and reporting on program finances
  • Publicizing the program

🎓 As a student, I am responsible for:

  • Attending class lectures and participating in reviews
  • Developing and documenting assigned projects
  • Honestly reporting my work and attributing others' work
  • Working safely at all times
  • Leaving workspaces in the same or better condition
  • Participating in the upkeep of the lab
  • Ensuring tuition and class costs are covered
  • Following health and safety guidance
  • Promoting a respectful, harassment-free environment
